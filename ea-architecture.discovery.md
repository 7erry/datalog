# Discovery: MongoDB Enterprise Advanced — architecture deep dive

**Companion:** `ea-architecture.html`

## Opening minute (discovery call)

Thanks—**Enterprise Advanced** is where regulated and large-scale teams ask hard questions: deployment topology, controls, auditability, and how MongoDB fits **next to** existing enterprise standards. I’ll map capabilities to **your non-negotiables**—not a feature tour for its own sake. Which constraints are loudest right now: **security/compliance**, **operational ownership**, or **performance at scale**?

## Why current approaches fall short (without a competitive move)

Enterprise Advanced matters when teams **under-buy controls**—then try to bolt audit, encryption, and ops rigor on late. Non-competitive is treating EA as a SKU checkbox instead of **mapping to control frameworks and ownership**.

## Cost of inaction

Inaction surfaces as **audit findings, delayed go-lives, and expensive exceptions**. Legal and security become **serial revisitors** because architecture evidence was never assembled. You pay for **rush implementations** and reworked designs under scrutiny.

## Ten open-ended questions (pain points & buying process)

1. What compliance regimes or internal control frameworks apply to this workload?
2. How do you audit database activity today, and what gaps show up in assessments?
3. Who operates MongoDB—employees, MSP, hybrid—and what SLAs do you hold them to?
4. What deployment patterns are mandated—on-prem, dedicated cloud, sovereign regions?
5. How does procurement treat enterprise software—SKUs, legal terms, support entitlements?
6. What technical proof does your security team need before production approval?
7. Who signs the business case—CIO, CISO, head of data—and what metrics matter to them?
8. What other vendors are in the “strategic” tier that influence MongoDB negotiations?
9. What renewal cycle are we inside, and what leverage or constraints does that create?
10. What would a successful EA rollout look like in your org twelve months from signature?
